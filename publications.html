My Publications

The academia is very close to my heart and I am doing my best to contribute to the ever-growing knowledge. My main research interests are Games User Research, User Experience, and Psychology of Computer Games with special care to motivation and negative experiences. I believe that we need a better understanding of how multimedia consumers use their devices, surf the web, play games, and interact with each other in the wild. We must observe both positive and negative motivational and emotional structures on micro and macro level. Only with this deep understanding of positive experiences, we will be able to communicate our ideas better. Only with knowing the structure and nature of negative experiences we can be prepared to design better games for the development and enjoyment of our players.
